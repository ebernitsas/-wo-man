NAME
       dump - dumps parter via email

SYNOPSIS
       dump  [-r reason]  [-n name]  [-mn your name]  [-e email] [-sp power wheel]
       [-sr abuse webpage]

DESCRIPTION
       takes information: reason, partner name, name and email and sends breakup em-
       ail to specified parter.  default reasons include mostlu abusive reasons due 
       to the known issue of women feelikg trapped in abusive relationships.

OPTIONS
       -r  reason
              If reason not included, one or more of the following can be used by t-
              heir number.

       -n  partner name
              Dear [partner name], will be included in email.

       -mn  name
              Best, [name] will be included in email.

       -e   email  
              Address to send letter to.

       -sp  show power wheel
              Shows wheel describing power struggles in abusive relationships.

       -sr  show abusing reasons
              Shows webpage describing the types of abuse specified in reasons.
